The venerable elm stood at the edge of the clearing, its gnarled limbs reaching skyward, foliage murmuring tales of summers gone. Passersby halted beneath its canopy, longing relief from the harsh sunlight. Under its bowers, blossoms painted the ground in soft pastels, and the breeze bore echoes of rippling water and distant song. Every wanderer departed with a quiet joy, their spirits lifted by the elm’s gentle presence.
